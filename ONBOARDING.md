# Join a Sandoq circle — 5 minutes, no real money

Sandoq runs on the Stellar **testnet**, so everything here uses free test XLM.
Nothing costs anything and no real funds are ever involved.

## 1. Get a Stellar wallet

The easiest is **Freighter**, a browser extension: <https://freighter.app>

Install it, create a wallet, and **write down your recovery phrase**.

## 2. Switch it to Testnet

Open Freighter → the network dropdown at the top → choose **Test Net**.

## 3. Get free test XLM

In Freighter on Test Net you'll see a **"Fund with Friendbot"** button — click it.
(Or fund your address at <https://lab.stellar.org/account/fund>.) You'll get
10,000 test XLM, which is plenty.

## 4. Open the app and connect

Go to **<https://itsgriznft.github.io/sandoq/>** and click **Connect wallet →
Freighter**, then approve.

## 5. Join a circle

Pick any circle marked **Filling** and press **Join**. Freighter will ask you to
sign — approve it. Joining stakes a small refundable collateral (5–10 test XLM)
and takes your seat.

- When the circle fills up it **starts on its own**. Each round, press
  **Contribute** to pay in; the whole pot rotates to one member at a time.
- Your collateral comes back with **Reclaim** when the circle finishes — minus
  only what would cover a round you skipped.

## 6. Tell us what you think

Tap **Give feedback** in the footer — an emoji and one line is plenty. It helps
shape what Sandoq builds next.

---

**What's happening under the hood:** joining, contributing and settling are all
real transactions on Stellar testnet, signed by your own wallet. No organizer —
not even us — can touch the money; the smart contract holds every stake and pays
every pot. You can verify any transaction on
[Stellar Expert](https://stellar.expert/explorer/testnet).
